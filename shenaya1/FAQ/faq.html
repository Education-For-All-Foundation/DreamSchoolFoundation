Frequently Asked Questions - (FAQs)

Why should I donate through Dream School Foundation?

Appealing to parents, the school community, and local businesses is a fantastic and increasingly popular way to raise the funds needed for those on-going campaigns and "nice to haves" that make can make a school an even more enjoyable place to learn - especially when current budgets may only stretch just enough to cover your education provision.

Why should I consider raising funds for my school?

Due to changes to the National Funding Formula, many schools are finding that current budgets only just stretch to the education provision. We give you the chance to appeal to directly to the school’s own community including parents, grandparents, former pupils, and even beyond to local or not so local businesses to help fund inspirational campaigns like a stunning new IT suite, or to accept regular donations to fund on-going campaigns such school trips or to provide the best classroom equipment.

What is your Basic Service?

Our basic service provides you with all of the tools to run your own successful crowdfunding campaign, and to collect donations through our secure online portal, including:

- A full suite of tools to help spread the word of your campaign
- A dedicated campaign webpage including photos and even a video
- Easy, one-click social media sharing including Facebook and Twitter

How does my donation get to the school?

We transfer regular donations and Gift Aid for regular campaigns directly to the school on a monthly basis. Where a school is running a project specific campaign then we transfer funds when the fundraising target for the campaign is successfully pledged.

How do we create the fundraising campaign webpage?

Our secure platform makes it easy for you to upload your school’s logo, photos and appeal text to create a great looking webpage for your fundraising campaign. You can even provide a video to tell your audience about your school’s campaign.
