Project methodology

Before beginning any project, our foundation undertakes an extensive research process that intends to understand a particular issue, and how best to respond whilst continuing to honour the foundationâ€™s values.

This includes ensuring partners meet a particular standard and are not on any sanction list. It means our foundation strives to understand and work closely with select partners that share their values and goals.

All projects are designed with the beneficiary in mind, with people on the ground at locations globally ensuring that projects operate only to the benefit of beneficiaries. It means the organisation strives to understand their needs and the best means by which to meet them.

It all amounts to an appraisal that meets only the highest of the organisations standards, and ensures that all potential risks are mitigated or managed to the best of our abilities, before the project is fundraised and delivered.

This entire process is to ensure that all projects under our foundation meet the high standards the organisation sets itself, as well as providing accountability and transparency to our donors.
