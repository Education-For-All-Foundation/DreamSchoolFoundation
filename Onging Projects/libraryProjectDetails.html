Library project details of Hali-Ela Central College

This project is to implement on the premise that libraries with a good collection of wide-ranging books will promote self-study and enhance the minds of children.

The main objective of this project include providing opportunities to underprivileged children in Hali Ela Central College to enhance knowledge and derive the simple pleasure of exploring the world through books with a special emphasis on stimulating a love of reading and learning in children, promote international and inter-cultural understanding and awareness, improve and expand knowledge of English thus creating opportunities for higher education and employment to support and facilitate the teaching and learning activities of the School, by providing access to appropriate literature and information resources and to provide an appropriate and comfortable environment, accommodation and facilities for the use of Library resources, and for individual and group.

Our Team members have willingly undertaken to visit schools off the beaten path and spent time with the teaching staff and parents to garner commitment for the project.
We have paid attention to the project in its entirety without merely donating a few books. It involves renovating and furnishing a proper library facility for the children. Our team maintains close relationships with the school to ensure the continuity of the project. Maintenance work is carried out every year and the libraries are stocked with fresh collections on an annual basis. In addition to providing books worth millions of rupees on a diverse range of topics, book lists provided by the schools are supplied to meet the individual needs of each school.
